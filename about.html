About Us

Some in-depth detail of our work

Welcome to The Programming Club at BMSIT&M. We, at TPC, as we like to call ourselves aim at providing solutions for exciting challenges that we face each day.

TPC aims at creating a base of strong fundamentals of basic programming by methods which are different than droning lectures.

To keep things competitive and spirited, there are events like Hackathons, Guest Lectures from Industrial Experts and much more.

What's more? We help you with ideas for mini projects and help you get through with it. We discuss new ideas and convert them into reality at this club!

So, if you want to showcase your inner geek or make the world better, do stop by at our session.
