About Us

Some in-depth detail of our work

Welcome to The Programming Club. We, at TPC, as we like to call ourselves aim at providing one cut throat solution for most the commonly arising technological queries.

TPC aims at creating a base of strong fundamentals of basic programming by methods which are different than the droning lectures.

To keep things competitive and spirited, there will be events like Hackathons, Guest Lectures from Industrial Experts and much more.

What's more? We will help you with ideas for mini projects and help you get through with it. We will discuss new ideas and convert them into reality within this club!

So, all we would like to say is we are the place to go for everything tech-y.
